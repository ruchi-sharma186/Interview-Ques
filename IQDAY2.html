Ques35.Why is a URL encoded in HTML?

A URL is encoded in HTML to ensure special characters are safely transmitted over the web, as certain characters (like spaces or symbols) could interfere with the URL structure or be misinterpreted. URL encoding replaces these characters with a percent sign (%) followed by their ASCII code.
